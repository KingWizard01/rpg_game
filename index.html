Murders of Grubennstein

Welcome to the game! You are a detective who has been hired to investigate a series of murders in the small town of Grubennstein. Local plebs blame everyone from vampires and werewolves to witches and politicians.

But will you be clean and honest in your pursuit of justice? Or will you accept corrupt coin and promises of power so long as you cover up the truth?
